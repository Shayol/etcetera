etcetera

etcetera is a design studio.

We create digital interfaces, branding solutions and creative direction for cultural institutions, arts organizations, museums, leading global brands and businesses.

Some of our commissions include the creative direction, visual identity and web development of Tiempo Circular in collaboration with Espacio Pla and The Wrong, an interactive video installation at MALBA and ongoing art direction of Warp Records, collaboration with fashion brand Origami, and multiple publications designed for HILO.
